Welcome to the official blog of **BasaltPass**!

We are excited to introduce BasaltPass, a modern, secure, and multi-tenant authentication system designed to simplify identity management for your applications.

<!--truncate-->

## Why BasaltPass?

In today's digital landscape, security and scalability are paramount. Building a robust authentication system from scratch is complex and time-consuming. BasaltPass provides a ready-to-use solution that is:

*   **Multi-Tenant by Design**: Seamlessly manage multiple organizations with strict data isolation.
*   **Standards Compliant**: Fully compatible with OAuth 2.0 and OpenID Connect (OIDC).
*   **Secure**: Implements best practices like PKCE and RBAC out of the box.
*   **Developer Focused**: Easy to integrate with comprehensive documentation and APIs.
